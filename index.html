Welcome to Out & Up! We are an outdoor activity centre in the south west of Oldham targeting young adults. We run a multitude of engaging, lively activities such as:

  • Zip-wire
  • Go carting
  • Obstacle course
  • And many more!

These activities are not for the faint-hearted however, as they require a lot of skill, teamwork, confidence and determination to complete. These life skills are the kind of the skills that businesses look for!
